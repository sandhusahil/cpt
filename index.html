Final Pillar List

A concise breakdown of each pillar and its explicit paths.

Understand Myself

  1. Self-Reflection — Regularly set aside time to reflect.
  2. Self-Awareness — Identify values & triggers.
  3. Personality Exploration — Use assessments to learn patterns.

  3. Healthy Eating — Balanced diet.
  4. Mental Fitness — Cognitive exercises.
  5. Adequate Rest — 7–8 hours; bedtime 10:00–10:30 pm.

Accountability & Enforcement

  1. Minor Violations — 5-min meditation + 10-min walk next day.
  2. Major Violations — 1-hour No Phone Zone.
  3. Extreme Violations — Full entertainment ban next day.
  4. Rewards — 7-day ₹100 treat, 14-day movie, 30-day useful purchase.
  5. Daily Accountability — Nightly 3-question check.
